History

White Water Rafting has been in business since 1984. We started out with only one run: Angel Falls. Our family business has since expanded to include rafting runs along Little Bend, Blue Lagoon, and Lazy River. Our most recent addition, Camping Run, is our most inclusive package, promising excitement and a greater connection with the great oudoors.

We are a family owned business with deep roots in our community. We boast extensive knowledge of the surrounding areas, and have experience in wilderness survival. White Water Rafting has even been involved in search and rescue missions in the past, putting those skills to the test in aiding our community. With our history and experience, you can be sure you are in good hands for the duration of your trip.
