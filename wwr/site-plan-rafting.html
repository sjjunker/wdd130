Rafting Site Plan

Sandi Junker

Overview

Purpose

Build revenue by attracting a larger customer base to the rafting company through the web. Goals include providing information on company location, availability, and pricing packages; as well as, creating an attractive site that positively advertises the rafting services offered.

Audience

Who are the Target customers? Young adults, families with older children, retreat groups, and other adventure seekers. What do they want? To find an exciting actvity, or adventure, to spend with their friends and/or family. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Information about what to expect on a trip, pricing, and any logistical or safety information like location, times available, age limitations, etc.
